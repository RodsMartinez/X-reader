PLOT

This takes place in the distant future where the protagonist wakes up in a futuristic solitary confinement. Here you stumble upon an android, that of which resembles the female body, who guides you the moment you wake up. She introduced herself as a bot dedicated to guiding people like you, she calls herself Hadley Therese M. Lewis, (HTML for short).

She explains that the world they live in has been in a constant war and has continuously been so for the past decades. You, however, are the key to ending this feud, they call you the XREADER. You, and only you, survived the experimentation, and are capable of traveling through the memories of your different past lives. But due to extensive experimentation you have forgotten the current person you originally were. Replacing it with the name the user provides.

It’s time travel but with mind swapping!

An important information was buried in the past and only you can acquire it. Your mission is to find the key that could end the war by accessing 5 past versions of yourself. Can you find the answer within those past 5 lives?
